Noise-Canceling Microphones

One of the most sought-after technologies for noise cancelling headphones is a dual microphone design that employs two microphones that are directional to block out the sounds of the surrounding. The primary microphone is situated close to the source of sound such as the user's voice or their music. The second microphone is set to record ambient sounds, such as traffic noise and other voices. When the two signals are subtracted from each other, a lot of ambient noise is cancelled out, leaving only the desired audio signal.

Another kind of headset with noise cancellation technology utilizes a hybrid system.
